Tomato Soup

INGREDIENTS

  • 4 cups ripe tomatoes, chopped
  • 1 medium onion, chopped
  • 2 cups vegetable broth
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

COOKING INSTRUCTIONS

  1. Heat olive oil in a pot over medium heat. Add chopped onion and saut√© until soft.
  2. Add chopped tomatoes and cook for about 5 minutes until they start breaking down.
  3. Pour in the vegetable broth and bring the mixture to a boil. Season with salt and pepper.
  4. Reduce heat and let it simmer for 20 minutes to develop the flavors.
  5. Blend the soup until smooth, then serve hot.
